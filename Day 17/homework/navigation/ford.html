Inforamation about Ford:


Ford Motor Company (commonly known as Ford) is an
American multinational automobile manufacturer
headquartered in Dearborn, Michigan, United States. It was
founded by Henry Ford and incorporated on June 16, 1903.
The company sells automobiles and commercial vehicles
under the Ford brand, and luxury cars under its Lincoln brand.
The company is listed on the New York Stock Exchange and is controlled by the Ford family;
they have minority ownership but the majority of the voting power.
