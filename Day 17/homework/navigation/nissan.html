Information about nissan:


Nissan Motor Corporation (日産自動車株式会社, Nissan
Jidōsha kabushiki gaisha) is a Japanese multinational
automobile manufacturer headquartered in Yokohama,
Kanagawa, Japan. The company sells its vehicles under the
Nissan and Infiniti brands, and formerly the Datsun brand, with
in-house performance tuning products (including cars) under
the Nismo and Autech brands. The company traces back to
the beginnings of the 20th century, with the Nissan zaibatsu,
now called Nissan Group.
